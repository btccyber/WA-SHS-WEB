Foundation and Early Years

Wa Senior High School was established in the 1970s as part of Ghana's educational expansion program to bring quality secondary education to the northern regions. From its humble beginnings, the school was envisioned to serve as a beacon of learning and character development in the Upper West Region.

The school's strategic location in Wa, the regional capital, made it accessible to students from across the Upper West Region and neighboring areas. Our founders believed in creating an institution that would not only provide academic excellence but also instill strong moral values in our students.

Over the years, Wa Senior High School has grown from a small institution to become one of the most respected secondary schools in northern Ghana, earning the proud title "Pride of the North."
